Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer delivery services? +

Yes, we offer delivery within a 20-mile radius of our farm. Delivery fees vary based on distance and order size. Free delivery is available for orders over $50.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. For in-person purchases, we also accept cash.

Are all your products certified organic? +

Yes, all our products are certified organic by the USDA. We follow strict organic farming practices and never use synthetic pesticides or fertilizers.

Can I visit the farm? +

We offer farm tours on select weekends throughout the year. Please check our events calendar or contact us to schedule a visit. Group tours are available by appointment.

Do you offer wholesale pricing? +

Yes, we offer wholesale pricing for restaurants, grocery stores, and other businesses. Please contact us directly to discuss your needs and set up an account.
