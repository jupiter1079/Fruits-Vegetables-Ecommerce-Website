Our Story

Two decades of passion for organic farming and community connection

Our Journey Timeline

2001

The Beginning

Jupiter Farms was founded with just 5 acres of land and a dream to provide our community with fresh, organic produce. What started as a small family operation quickly grew as neighbors discovered the superior quality of our farm-fresh products.

2008

Expanding Our Reach

We expanded our operations to include partnerships with other local farms who shared our values of sustainable agriculture. This allowed us to offer a wider variety of produce while maintaining our commitment to quality and ethical farming practices.

2015

Certified Organic

After years of following organic practices, we officially received our organic certification, validating our commitment to chemical-free farming and sustainable agriculture methods that prioritize soil health and biodiversity.

2020

Community Support During Challenging Times

During the pandemic, we implemented contactless delivery and expanded our subscription service to ensure our community had consistent access to fresh, healthy food. The overwhelming support from our customers reinforced our belief in the importance of local food systems.

Present

Looking to the Future

Today, Jupiter Farms continues to grow while staying true to our roots. We're exploring new sustainable farming techniques, expanding our educational programs, and finding innovative ways to connect our community with the source of their food.
