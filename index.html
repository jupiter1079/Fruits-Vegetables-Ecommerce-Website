Our Story

From Our Family to Yours

Jupiter Farms is a local, family-owned supplier of high-quality, organic, and locally-sourced fruits and vegetables. For over two decades, we've been providing our community with the freshest produce straight from our farm.

Our reputation is built on freshness, sustainability, and personalized customer service. We believe in transparent farming practices and building relationships with our customers.

We partner with local farms who share our values of sustainable agriculture and ethical farming practices. Together, we're strengthening our local food system.
