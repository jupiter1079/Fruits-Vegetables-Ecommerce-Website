Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer farm tours?

Yes! We offer guided farm tours every Saturday at 10am and 2pm. Tours are R100 per person and include a seasonal produce sample. Please book in advance as spaces are limited.

What are your delivery options?

We offer local delivery within 20km of our farm for orders over R500. Delivery is free for orders over R750. We also offer pickup at our farm stand during business hours.

Are you certified organic?

Yes, we are certified organic by the AGRI-SA. We've been following organic practices since our founding in 2001 and received official certification in 2015.
